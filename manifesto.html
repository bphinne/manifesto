Akin to a humanist manifesto[1], but geared towards the cute, the alternative, the colorful, and the grunge.

An exploration of how cute has no boundaries. No restrictions.

To explore the self through an artistic expression that almost fleetingly changes from cute to dark and gothic.

Inspired in youth by Camilla d’Errico’s Dappled Daydreams[2] and explorations of the cute and nonrealistic.

Anyone can learn, it’s just a matter of dedication and the passage of time.

I pour my soul into my work, using cuteness to disguise and convey how I feel.

It’s a powerful outlet, and one that needs no words spoken to understand.

I express the everyday, the emotions, and the passions of my life through cute imagery that transcends boundaries.

Maybe, you can try too..
